Filipino Desserts

Food and social media extends far beyond just my work life. To me, food is a way of bridging communities across different cultures, and my background as a Filipino-American is perpetually present in all the work that I do. Here is a small preview of a bigger project I worked on that focused on educating viewers on different varieties of Filipino desserts.

Cheeseboard Game

Engagement with viewers is an essential for community-building. That is what I was trying to accomplish with this project, which conceptualized around a fun “this or that?” game format with a trendy charcuterie theme. This is just a few slides of the larger project that has been touted by my team as one of our most successful IG stories, garnering 5.6 million views.

FLY Infographic

Fresh Lifelines for Youth (FLY) is a nonprofit that serves young people in the Bay Area that are impacted by the juvenile justice system. This is an animated infographic I designed for their Instagram and Facebook stories outlining the concept of the school-to-prison-pipeline in a simple and easily digestible manner.

EyeCandy Film Journal

As a student at the University of California, Santa Cruz, I was the Managing Editor of EyeCandy Film Journal. It was during my time as part of the Officer Board that we launched our very first social media account (Instagram) and I came up with this fun, interactive game to encourage fellow film aficionados to engage with our content.
